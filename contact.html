About

Sarah Adam is an interior decorator specializing in maximalist, layered interiors that are bold, livable, and deeply personal.

With a background in Fashion Merchandising from the Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT) in New York City, Sarah brings a trained eye for color, texture, proportion, and storytelling to every space she works on. Her design philosophy is rooted in the belief that a room should feel collected — not decorated — and that beauty lives in the details: an unexpected material, a saturated palette, a piece that stops you in your tracks.

Her aesthetic is unapologetically maximalist. She is drawn to spaces that feel curated over time, full of personality and intention, with layers that reward a second look.

In addition to her residential work, Sarah offers photorealistic room rendering services to homeowners and home and lifestyle brands alike — translating design concepts into vivid, high-fidelity visuals before a single piece is purchased or a single wall is painted. This service bridges the gap between imagination and execution, giving clients confidence and clarity at every stage of the design process.
